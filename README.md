# ColSoc Issues Tracker 🪲

Welcome to the ColSoc Issues Tracker! This repository serves as a centralized, collaborative hub for discussing taxonomy and identification issues for Coleoptera in the UK.

Whether you are a professional entomologist, a citizen scientist, or an amateur coleopterist, your contributions are welcome here.

## 🎯 What is this repository for?

Instead of storing code, we use GitHub's **Issue Tracker** to manage and discuss entomological data. We track three main things:
* **Identification Issues:** Issues relating to identification of species and data problems this may cause.
* **Taxonomic Corrections:** Discussing and logging updates to beetle nomenclature, synonymies, and reclassifications.
* **New Species Logs:** Tracking undescribed species and logging newly published taxa.

## 🛠️ How to Contribute

To contribute, simply click on the **[Issues](../../issues)** tab at the top of this repository and click **New Issue**. 

You will be presented with three templates to choose from:

1.  **🪲 Identification Issue:** Choose this if you come across an identification issue that has the potential to cause problems when recording.
2.  **📚 Taxonomy Correction:** Choose this if you are proposing an update to existing taxonomy.
3.  **🌟 New Species:** Choose this to get a new species onto the checklists.

## 🤝 Community Guidelines
* **Be respectful:** Treat all members with courtesy, whether they are publishing academics or beginners asking simple questions.
* **Cite your sources:** When discussing taxonomy, always link to the relevant scientific literature, catalogs, or databases (e.g., ZooBank, GBIF).
* **Keep it scientific:** While enthusiasm is encouraged, please ensure discussions remain focused on the science of coleopterology.
